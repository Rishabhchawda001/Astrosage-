## Known Limitations

1. **Verse counting uses line-based heuristic** — not semantic verse detection. Line counts exceed traditional verse counts because prose, commentary, and metadata lines are counted as "verse-like."

4. **BORI critical editions** of Mahabharata and other texts are copyrighted and cannot be redistributed.
5. **Combined files** (Upanishads_110.txt) contain multiple texts; section extraction may lose context.

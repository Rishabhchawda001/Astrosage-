The above Turiya Gayatri Mantra is considered to be very sacred. There are differing 
accounts as to whether this mantra can be chanted by one and all or it requires formal 
initiation into this mantra by a Sat Guru. But according to Srimad Devi Bhagavatham, this 
mantra must be chanted as part of daily Anushtanam (Sandhavandana) immediately after 
Gayatri Japa. Those who would like to chant this mantra are advised to seek the guidance of 
eminent scholars.

note that this mantra should NEVER be chanted without proper initiation through a 
Sat Guru.

1000th verse. Those 24 slokas which contain the Sri. Gayatri Mantra aksharas are known as 
Gayatri Ramayanam and are given below. It is believed that chanting Gayatri Ramayanam 
gives the benefit of chanting both Gayatri Mantra and Valmiki Ramayana.

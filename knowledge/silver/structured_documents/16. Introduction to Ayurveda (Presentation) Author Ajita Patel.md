what is Ayurveda? 
Science or knowledge of life, sister science to yoga. 
Mother of all healing systems (TCM,homeopathy, 
Allopathic,and naturopathy). 
Timeless wisdom practiced continuously for over 5000 
years.

Complete body – mind –spirit approach to health and 
well being using natural therapies.

our divinity and live in harmony with laws of nature.


=== Chapter 1 ===

parameśaṃ namaskṛtya bharadvājamṛṣiṃ tataḥ 
harādindrakramāyātaṃ jñānaṃ śṛṇuta suvratāḥ

nārāyaṇāśrame puṇye bharadvājādayo dvijāḥ 
tepuḥ śivaṃ pratiṣṭhāpya tadekāhitamānasāḥ

atha tānbhāvitānmatvā kadācittridaśādhipaḥ 
tadāśramapadaṃ bheje svayaṃ tāpasaveṣabhṛt

sa taiḥ saṃpūjitaḥ pṛṣṭvā tāṃśca sarvānanāmayam 
provāca codanādharmaḥ kimarthaṃ nānuvartyate

ta ūcurnanvayaṃ dharmaś codanāvihito mune 
devatārādhanopāyas tapasābhīṣṭasiddhaye

vede 'sti saṃhitā raudrī vācyā rudraśca devatā 
sānnidhyakaraṇe 'pyasmin vihitaḥ kālpiko vidhiḥ

ityukte 'pi paraṃ bhāvaṃ jijñāsuḥ prahasanprabhuḥ 
tānāha mithyā jñānaṃ vaḥ śabdamātraṃ hi devatā

śabdetaratve yugapadbhinnadeśeṣu yaṣṭṛṣu 
na sā prayāti sāṃnidhyaṃ mūrtatvadasmadādivat

na ca tatsādhakaṃ kiṃcit pramāṇaṃ bhātyabādhitam 
vākyaṃ tadanyathāsiddhaṃ lokavādāḥ kva sādhavaḥ

ityanīśavacovārivelānunno 'bdhineva saḥ 
śakreṇa na cacālaiṣāṃ dhīśailaḥ sāragauravāt

na jātu devatāmūrtir asmadādiśarīravat 
viśiṣṭaiśvaryasampannā sāto naitannidarśanam

athāstvevaṃ ghaṭe nyāyaḥ śabdatvādindraśabdavat 
nādatte ghaṭaśabdo 'mbhaś candraśabdo na rājate

athānyaviṣayaṃ vākyam astu śakrādivācakam 
karmarūpādiśabdānāṃ sārthakatvaṃ katham bhavet

pravādo 'pyakhilo mithyā samūlatvānna yuktimat 
na cedamūlaṃ bhūtānāṃ hatāḥ sarvāḥ pravṛttayaḥ

upamanyurharaṃ dṛṣṭvā vimanyurabhavanmuniḥ 
kathaṃ tasya vaco mithyā yasya vaśyaḥ payonidhiḥ

kroḍīkṛto 'hipāśena viṣajvālāvalīmucā 
huṅkṛtya mocitaḥ patyā dṛṣṭaḥ śveto dhanairjanaiḥ

iti vādānuṣaṅgeṇa haraśaṃsāpraharṣitān 
sāśrugadgadavācastān vīkṣya prīto 'bhavaddhariḥ

svaṃ rūpaṃ darśayāmāsa vajrī devaḥ śatakratuḥ 
taruṇādityasaṃkāśaṃ stūyamānaṃ marudgaṇaiḥ

te tamṛgbhiryajurbhiśca sāmabhiścāstuvannatāḥ 
so 'bravīducyatāṃ kāmo jagatsu pravaro 'pi yaḥ

te vavrire śivajñānaṃ śrūyatāmiti so 'bravīt 
kiṃtveko 'stu mama praṣṭā nikhilaśrotṛsammataḥ

atha teṣāṃ bharadvājo bhagavānagraṇīrabhūt 
vāgmī pragalbhaḥ papraccha nyāyataḥ surapūjitam

kathaṃ maheśvarādetad āgataṃ jñānamuttamam 
kiṃ ca cetasi saṃsthāpya nirmame bhagavānidam

sṛṣṭikāle maheśānaḥ puruṣārthaprasiddhaye 
vidhatte vimalaṃ jñānaṃ pañcasroto 'bhilakṣitam

tadvartivācakavrātavācyānaṣṭau maheśvarān 
saptakoṭiprasaṃkhyātān mantrāṃśca parame 'dhvani

aṣṭādaśādhikaṃ cānyac chrutaṃ māyādhikāriṇām 
mantreśvarāṇāmūrdhvādhvasthiteśopamatejasām

teṣu vyaktaḥ sa bhagavān idaṃ yogyeṣu siddhaye 
prakāśayatyato 'nyeṣu yo 'rthaḥ samupapadyate

śivodgīrṇamidaṃ jñānaṃ mantramantreśvareśvaraiḥ 
kāmadatvātkāmiketi pragītaṃ bahuvistaram

tebhyo 'vagatya dṛgjyotir jvālālīḍhasmaradrumaḥ 
dadāvumāpatirmahyaṃ sahasrairbhavasaṃmitaiḥ

tatrāpi vistaraṃ hitvā sūtraiḥ sārārthavācakaiḥ 
vakṣye nirākulaṃ jñānaṃ taduktaireva bhūyasā

athānādimalāpetaḥ sarvakṛtsarvavicchivaḥ 
pūrvavyatyāsitasyāṇoḥ pāśajālamapohati

tripadārthaṃ catuṣpādaṃ mahātantraṃ jagatpatiḥ 
sūtreṇaikena saṃhṛtya prāha vistaraśaḥ punaḥ

jagajjanmasthitidhvaṃsatirobhāvavimuktayaḥ 
kṛtyaṃ sakārakaphalaṃ jñeyamasyaitadeva hi

tena svabhāvasiddhena bhavitavyaṃ jagatkṛtā 
arvāksiddhe 'navasthā syān mokṣo nirhetuko 'pi vā

caitanyaṃ dṛkkriyārūpaṃ tadastyātmani sarvadā 
sarvataśca yato muktau śrūyate sarvatomukham

sadapyabhāsamānatvāt tanniruddhaṃ pratīyate 
vaśyo 'nāvṛtavīryasya so 'ta evāvimokṣaṇāt

prāvṛtīśabale karma māyākāryaṃ caturvidham 
pāśajālaṃ samāsena dharmā nāmnaiva kīrtitāḥ

iti vastutrayasyāsya prākpādakṛtasaṃsthiteḥ 
caryāyogakriyāpādair viniyogo 'bhidhāsyate

viniyogaphalaṃ muktir bhuktirapyanuṣaṅgataḥ 
parāparavibhāgena bhidyete te tvanekadhā

vedānasāṃkhyasadasatpādārthikamatādiṣu 
sasādhanā muktirasti ko viśeṣaḥ śivāgame

praṇetrasarvadarśitvān na sphuṭo vastusaṃgrahaḥ 
upāyāḥ saphalāstadvac chaive sarvamidaṃ param

vedānteṣveka evātmā cidacidvyaktilakṣitaḥ 
pratijñāmātramevedaṃ niścayaḥ kiṃnibandhanaḥ

atha pramāṇaṃ tatrātmā prameyatvaṃ prapadyate 
yatraitadubhayaṃ tatra catuṣṭayamapi sthitam

advaitahānirevaṃ syān niṣpramāṇakatānyathā 
bhogasāmyāvimokṣau ca yau neṣṭāvātmavādibhiḥ

sāṃkhyajñāne 'pi mithyātvaṃ kārye kāraṇabuddhitaḥ 
akartṛbhāvādbhoktuśca svātantryādapyacittvataḥ

iha sapta padārthāḥ syur jīvājīvāstravāstrayaḥ 
saṃvaro nirjaraścaiva bandhamokṣāvubhāvapi

syādvādalāñchitāścaite sarve 'naikāntikatvataḥ 
tadeva sattadevāsad iti kena pramīyate

sadanyadasadanyacca tadevaṃ siddhasādhyatā 
asajjaghanyaṃ sacchreṣṭham ityapi bruvate budhāḥ

naikatra tadapekṣātaḥ sthitamevobhayaṃ tataḥ 
atha cetsadasadbhāvaḥ sadāyuktataro mataḥ

tatkarmasaṃkarabhayād avyāpitvaṃ ca te jaguḥ 
sāmānyetarasambandhajñānābhāvādacetasaḥ

yaḥ prāgavyāpakaḥ so 'nte kathamanyādṛśo bhavet 
sa vikāsādidharmā cet tato doṣaparamparā

ṣaṭpadārthaparijñānān mithyājñānaṃ nivartate 
rāgadveṣau mamatvaṃ ca tadviśeṣaguṇāstataḥ

kramaśo vinivartante dehasaṃyogajā yataḥ 
sā muktirjaḍatārūpā tato muktaḥ śavo na kim

cidvyañjakasya karmādeḥ kṣaṇikatvānmuhurmuhuḥ 
vyajyate jāyamānaiva kṣaṇiketi matā paraiḥ

tadasatkarmaṇo bhogād atītānubhavasmṛteḥ 
sthitirniranvaye nāśe na smṛternāpi karmaṇaḥ

vināśalakṣaṇo 'paiti na muktāvapyupaplavaḥ 
na cāstyanubhavaḥ kaścid bhavāvasthā varaṃ tataḥ

ityādyajñānamūḍhāṇāṃ matamāśrityadurdhiyaḥ 
apavargamabhīpsanti khadyotātpāvakārthinaḥ

yatkaivalyaṃ puṃsprakṛtyor vivekād yo vā sarvaṃ brahma matvā virāmaḥ 
yā vā kāścinmuktayaḥ pāśajanyās tāstāḥ sarvā bhedamāyānti sṛṣṭau

śaive siddho bhāti mūrdhnītareṣāṃ muktaḥ sṛṣṭau punarabhyeti nādhaḥ 
viśvānarthānsvena viṣṭabhya dhāmnā sarveśānānīśitaḥ sarvadāste

athopalabhya dehādi vastu kāryatvadharmakam 
kartāramasya jānīmo viśiṣṭamanumānataḥ

vaiśiṣṭyaṃ kāryavaiśiṣṭyād dṛṣṭaṃ lokasthitāvapi 
This last half-line appears as part of the commentary (Ked p.104, lines 4--5)
but it probably belongs to the text and is quoted as part of it
in Trilocana's Siddhāntārthasamuccaya (IFP MS T.206, p.62 and T.284, p.133).
yadyathā yādṛśaṃ yāvat kāryaṃ tatkāraṇaṃ tathā 
nityaṃ kālānavacchedād dvaitatyānna pradeśagam

kramākramasamutpatteḥ kramādyutpattiśaktimat 
tasyāsti karaṇaṃ yena dṛṣṭā nākaraṇā kṛtiḥ

anāgāmi ca tajjñeyaṃ kāryasyānādisaṃsthiteḥ 
karaṇaṃ ca na śaktyanyac chaktirnācetanā citaḥ

viṣayāniyamādekaṃ bodhe kṛtye ca tattathā 
kāryaṃ na sthitijanmādi bījasya prakṛteraṇoḥ

pāriśeṣyānmaheśasya muktasya śiva eva saḥ 
sambandhāgrahaṇe bādhā mānasyābhyeti kasyacit

sā parasyāpi dhūmo 'nyo girau māhānasādyataḥ 
loke vapuṣmato dṛṣṭaṃ kṛtyaṃ so 'pyasmadādivat

mūlādyasambhavācchāktaṃ vapurno tādṛśaṃ prabhoḥ 
tadvapuḥ pañcabhirmantraiḥ pañcakṛtyopayogibhiḥ

īśatatpuruṣāghoravāmājairmastakādikam 
īṣṭe yena jagatsarvaṃ guṇenoparivartinā

sa mūrdhasamadeśatvān mūrdhā nāvayavastanoḥ 
tasya tasya tanuryā pūs tasyāmuṣati yena saḥ

tattrāṇādvyañjanāccāpi sa tatpuruṣavaktrakaḥ 
hṛdayaṃ bodhaparyāyaḥ so 'syāghoraḥ śivo yataḥ

parigrahasya ghoratvād ghoroktirupacārataḥ 
vāmastrivargavāmatvād rahasyaśca svabhāvataḥ 
vāmaṃ dhāma paraṃ guhyaṃ yasyāsau vāmaguhyakaḥ

sadyo 'ṇūnāṃ mūrtayaḥ sambhavanti yasyecchātastena sadyo 'bhidhānaḥ 
sadyo mūrtīryogināṃ vā vidhatte sadyomūrtiḥ kṛtyaśaighryānna mūrteḥ

itthaṃ śaktiḥ kurvatī dehakṛtyaṃ dehābhāvāducyate dehaśabdaiḥ 
tasyā bhedā ye 'pi vāmādayaḥ syus te 'pi proktāḥ kṛtyabhedena sadbhiḥ

sa itthaṃvigraho 'nena karaṇenāhataujasā 
karoti sarvadā kṛtyaṃ yadā yadupapadyate

tatrādau kevalāṇūnāṃ yogyānāṃ kurute 'ṣṭakam 
vāmādiśaktibhiryuktaṃ saptakoṭiparicchadam

teṣāmanantaḥ sūkṣmaśca tathā caiva śivottamaḥ 
ekanetraikarudrau ca trimūrtiścāmitadyutiḥ

śrīkaṇṭhaśca śikhaṇḍī ca rājarājeśvareśvarāḥ 
īṣadaprāptayogatvān niyojyāḥ parameṣṭhinaḥ

sarvajñatvādiyoge 'pi niyojyatvaṃ malāṃśataḥ 
parasparaṃ viśiṣyante mantrāścaivamadhaḥ sthitāḥ

te ca mantreśvaravyaktaśivaśaktipracoditāḥ 
kurvantyanugrahaṃ puṃsāṃ yadā yeṣāṃ sa yujyate

prayoktṛdehasāpekṣaṃ tadardhamakhile 'dhvani 
kṛtvādhikāraṃ sthityante śivaṃ viśati seśvaram

vinādhikaraṇenānyat pradhānavikṛteradhaḥ 
kṛtvādhikāramīśeṣṭam apaiti svādhvasaṃhṛtau

tato 'nantādyabhivyaktaḥ patīnāṃ granthitattvataḥ 
kalādyārabdhadehānāṃ karotyaṣṭādaśaṃ śatam

tānapyāviśya bhagavān sāñjanān bhuvanādhipān 
yebhyaḥ sarvamidaṃ yeṣāṃ śaktiḥ karmanibandhanā

praṇetṝṇ paśuśāstrāṇāṃ paśūṃstadanuvartakān 
svasādhyakārakopetān kāladhāmāvadhisthitān

sthitau sakārakānetān samākramya svatejasā 
yunakti svārthasiddhyarthaṃ bhūtairanabhilakṣitaḥ

bhogasādhanamākṣipya kṛtvā kāraṇasaṃśrayam 
tacca sātmakamākramya viśramāyāvatiṣṭhate

bhavināṃ bhavakhinnānāṃ sarvabhūtahito yataḥ 
svāpāvasānamāsādya punaḥ prāgvatpravartate

svāpe 'pyāste bodhayanbodhayogyān rodhyānrundhanpācayan karmikarma 
māyāśaktīrvyaktiyogyāḥ prakurvan paśyansarvaṃ yadyathā vastujātam

tamaḥśaktyadhikārasya nivṛttestatparicyutau 
vyanakti dṛkkriyānantyaṃ jagadbandhuraṇoḥ śivaḥ

yānvimocayati svāpe śivāḥ sadyo bhavanti te 
saṃhṛtau vā samudbhūtāv aṇavaḥ patayo 'thavā

rudramantrapatīśānapadabhājo bhavanti te 
sthitau yānanugṛhṇāti gurumāsthāya cidvataḥ

yeṣāṃ śarīriṇāṃ śaktiḥ patatyapi nivṛttaye 
teṣāṃ talliṅgamautsukyaṃ muktau dveṣo bhavasthitau

bhaktiśca śivabhakteṣu śraddhā tacchāsake vidhau 
anenānumitiḥ śiṣṭahetoḥ sthūladhiyāmapi

paśudṛgyogasiddhānāṃ karmavyaktidvayaṃ samam 
jyeṣṭhādiphalayogyānāṃ sādhikārāsu muktiṣu

upāyādaravaiśiṣṭyān mṛgyate tattrayaṃ punaḥ 
dvayorvyaktikaraḥ kaścic cyutisiddhivilakṣitaḥ

īṣadardhanivṛtte tu rodhakatve tamaḥpateḥ 
bhavantyetāni liṅgāni kiṃcicchiṣṭe ca dehinām

yogyatātrayamapyetat samatītya maheśvaraḥ 
svāpe 'numanugṛhṇāti sādhikāramidaṃ yataḥ

sargamūle tṛtīyāyāṃ svāpavadbhūtasaṃhṛtau 
sa yadvyapāsya kriyate tadvidho yo 'ṇurucyate

tathā bījaṃ śarīrādeḥ pācayatyāniveśanāt 
na yogyatāṅgamabhajat sadyaḥ syādauṣadhādivat

pākārhamapi tatpaktuṃ neśatyātmānamātmanā 
dharmasāmānya evāyaṃ sarvasya pariṇāminaḥ

sarvajñaḥ sarvakartṛtvāt sādhanāṅgaphalaiḥ saha 
yo yajjānāti kurute sa tadeveti susthitam

taccāsyāvṛtiśūnyatvān na vyañjakamapekṣate 
tanna sāṃśayikaṃ tasmād viparītaṃ na jātucit

yāni vyañjakamīkṣante vṛtatvānmalaśaktibhiḥ 
vyañjakasyānurodhena tāni syurvyāhatānyapi

nādhyakṣaṃ nāpi tallaiṅgaṃ na śābdamapi śāṅkaram 
jñānamābhāti vimalaṃ sarvadā sarvavastuṣu

tadekaṃ viṣayānantyād bhedānantyaṃ prapadyate 
kartṛtvaṃ tadabhinnatvāt tadvadevopacārataḥ

sattasvarūpakaraṇārthavidheyadṛgbhir leśoditābhiriti ye vidurīśatattvam 
te mocayanti bhavino bhavapaṅkamagnān no vistareṇa puruṣāḥ paśupāśarūpam

atha viśvanimittasya prāptaṃ lakṣaṇamātmanaḥ 
tadīśoktau gataprāyaṃ tathāpyuddeśa ucyate

kāryaṃ kṣityādi karteśas tatkarturnopayujyate 
na svārthamapyacidbhāvān nānarthyaṃ kartṛgauravāt

pāriśeṣyātparārthaṃ tat kṣetrajñaḥ sa parastayoḥ 
paro dehastadarthatvāt parārthāḥ kṣmādayo nanu

kāyo 'pyacittvādānyārthyaṃ sutarāṃ pratipadyate 
cetanaścenna bhogyatvād vikāritvācca jātucit

bhogyā vikāriṇo dṛṣṭāś cidvihīnāḥ paṭādayaḥ 
yasminsati ca sattvādvā na satyapi śave citiḥ

pariṇāmasay vaiśiṣṭyād asti cet na smṛtistadā 
nāpyevaṃ supratītatvāt smartā kāyetaro 'styataḥ

nāvyāpako na kṣaṇiko naiko nāpi jaḍātmakaḥ 
nākartā bhinnacidyogī pāśānte śivatāśruteḥ

athāvidyādayaḥ pāśāḥ kathyante leśato 'dhunā 
yeṣāmapāye patayo bhavanti jagato 'ṇavaḥ

pāśābhāve pāratantryaṃ vaktavyaṃ kinnibandhanam 
svābhāvikaṃ cenmukteṣu muktaśabdo nivartate

bandhaśūnyasya vaśitā dṛṣṭā baddhasya vaśyatā 
etāvatī te baddhatvamuktatve baddhamuktayoḥ

tatpāratantryaṃ baddhatvaṃ tasminnitye cidādivat 
muktisādhanasaṃdoho vyartho 'lamanayā dhiyā

nityavyāpakacicchaktinidhirapyarthasiddhaye 
pāśavaṃ śāmbhavaṃ vāpi nānviṣyatyanyathā balam

tadāvaraṇamasyāṇoḥ pañcasrotasi śāṅkare 
paryāyairbahubhirgītam adṛṣṭaṃ paśubhiḥ sadā

paśutvapaśunīhāramṛtyumūrcchāmalāñjanaiḥ 
avidyāvṛtirugglānipāpamūlakṣapādibhiḥ

tadekaṃ sarvabhūtānām anādi nibiḍaṃ mahat 
pratyātmasthasvakālāntāpāyiśaktisamūhavat

tadanādisthamarvāgvā taddhetustadato 'nyathā 
ruṇaddhi muktānevaṃ cen mokṣe yatnastato mṛṣā

tadekaṃ bahusaṃkhyaṃ tu tādṛgutpattimadyataḥ 
kintu tacchaktayo 'nekā yugapanmuktyadarśanāt

tāsāṃ māheśvarī śaktiḥ sarvānugrāhikā śivā 
dharmānuvartanādeva pāśa ityupacaryate

pariṇāmayatyetāśca rodhāntaṃ kārkacittviṣā 
yadonmīlanamādhatte tadānugrāhikocyate

śambhościdādyanugrāhyaṃ tadvirodhitayā mithaḥ 
yugapanna kṣamaṃ śaktiḥ sarvānugrāhikā katham

kathaṃ bhūtopakārārthaṃ pravṛttasya jagatprabhoḥ 
apakārakamāviśya yujyate tunnatodanam

na todanāya kurute malasyāṇoranugraham 
kintu yatkriyate kiñcit tadupāyena nānyathā

na sādhikāre tamasi muktirbhavati kasyacit 
adhikāro 'pi tacchakteḥ pariṇāmānnivartate

so 'pi na svata eva syād api yogyasya vastunaḥ 
sarvathā sarvadā yasmāc citprayojyamacetanam

yathā kṣārādinā vaidyas tudannapi na rogiṇam 
koṭāviṣṭārthadāyitvād duḥkhahetuḥ pratīyate

sarvagatvānmaheśasya nādhiṣṭhānaṃ vihanyate 
na ca yatrāsti kartavyaṃ tasminnaudāsyameti saḥ

dharmiṇo 'nugraho nāma yattaddharmānuvartanam 
na so 'sti kasyacijjātu yaḥ patyā nānuvartate

gatādhikāranīhāravīryasya sata edhate 
paśoranugraho 'nyasya tādarthyādasti karmaṇaḥ

boddhṛtvapariṇāmitvadharmayoranuvartanam 
malasya sādhikārasya nivṛttestatparicyutau

ityevaṃ yaugapadyena kramātsughata eva hi 
māyāyāḥ sādhikārāyāḥ karmaṇaścokta eva saḥ

athendriyaśarīrārthaiś cidyogasyānumīyate 
nimittamāgāmibhāvād yato nāgāmyahetumat

tasya pradeśavartitvād vaicitryātkṣaṇikatvataḥ 
pratipuṃniyatatvācca santatatvācca tadguṇam

īśāvidyādyapekṣitvāt sahakāri taducyate 
karma vyāpārajanyatvād adṛṣṭaṃ sūkṣmabhāvataḥ

janakaṃ dhārakaṃ bhogyam adhyātmāditrisādhanam 
tatsatyānṛtayonitvād dharmādharmasvarūpakam

svāpe vipākamabhyeti tatsṛṣṭāvupayujyate 
māyāyāṃ vartate cānte nābhuktaṃ layameti ca

iti māyādikālāntapravartakamanādimat 
karma vyañjakamapyetad rodhi sadyanna muktaye

atha sarvajñavākyena pratipannasya lakṣaṇam

athātmavatāṃ matvā [vā] svādhikāraṃ suduṣkaram 
yaterannatmavattāyai deśikādyā jigīṣavaḥ

tadātmavattvaṃ yogitvaṃ jitākṣasyopapadyate 
prāṇāyāmādyanuṣṭhānāj jitākṣatvaṃ śanaiḥ śanaiḥ

prāṇāyāmaḥ pratyāhāro dhāraṇā dhyānavīkṣaṇe 
japaḥ samādhirityaṅgāny aṅgī yogo 'ṣṭamaḥ svayam

prāṇaḥ prāgudito vāyur āyāmo 'sya prakhedanam 
preraṇākṛṣṭisaṃrodhalakṣaṇaṃ kratudoṣanut

tataḥ sukhalavāsvāde teṣāṃ vṛttasya cetasaḥ 
pratyāhāro vidhātavyaḥ sarvato vinivartanam

tenendriyārthasaṃsargavinivṛtteścito matiḥ 
dhāraṇāyogyatāmeti pade svecchāprakalpite

cintā tadviṣayā dhyānaṃ taccādiṣṭaṃ muhurmuhuḥ 
tadekatānatāmeti sa samādhirvidhīyate

japastadbhāṣaṇaṃ dhyeyasaṃmukhīkaraṇaṃ mune 
ūho 'bhivīkṣaṇaṃ vastuvikalpānantaroditaḥ

yadā vetti padaṃ heyam upādeyaṃ ca tatsthiteḥ 
tatpoṣakaṃ vipakṣaṃ ca yacca tatpoṣakaṃ param

eṣu vyastasamasteṣu kṛtayatnasya yoginaḥ 
vibhānti śaktayo viśvaṃ vyāpya bhānoriva tviṣaḥ

na tamīṣṭe naraḥ kaścit rakṣodānavamānavāḥ 
rorucānamatītyaitān dṛkkriyāprāṇarociṣā

nivṛttermanaso hetuḥ saṃsargātprāṇakhedanam 
nivṛttirdhāraṇādīnāṃ mūlaṃ sarvasya tattataḥ

vistareṇa suraśreṣṭha viprakṛṣṭaṃ ca yatsthitam 
yadanyatsādhanaṃ kiṃcid yogasiddheśca kathyatām

na śakyaṃ vistarādvaktuṃ tatprasaṅgabhayādvidheḥ 
praśnasyāvaśyavācyatvāt tathāpyuddeśa ucyate

mūtrādyutsṛjya vidhivad ekārdhadaśasaptabhiḥ 
mṛdbhirliṅgagudāsavyahastayugmāni śaucayet

dvirvratī trirapaḥ pītvā dvirvimṛjyānanaṃ spṛśet 
svabāhunābhihṛtkāni dvistrirvā śaucitādharaḥ

hitajīrṇāśanasvasthas trikuḍyāveṣṭite gṛhe 
bādhāśūnye vanādau vā svāsanastha udaṅmukhaḥ

namaskṛtya maheśānam umāskandagaṇādhipān 
ṛjugrīvāśirovakṣā nāsāgrāhitadṛgdvayaḥ

pārṣṇibhyāṃ vṛṣaṇau rakṣan dantairdantānasaṃspṛśan 
viṣṭabhadeho dantāgre jihvāmādāya susthitaḥ

recayecchaktiparyantaṃ puṭenaikena mārutam 
sa recakastadabhyāsād vedhavikṣiptakarmasu

kramaśaḥ śaktatāmeti vikṛṣṭaviṣayeṣvapi 
bāhyena vāyunā mūrteḥ śaktisīmaprapūraṇam

pūrakaḥ sa tadabhyāsāt sugurvapi vikarṣayet 
tyāgasaṃgrahaṇe hitvā nirodhaḥ kumbhakaḥ smṛtaḥ

rodhaśaktistadabhyāsād vyaktimetyanivāritā 
tathāsya carato vidvān somasūryeśavartmasu

taddharmayogyatāṃ buddhvā yogī saṃsādhayenmatam 
pūrakaṃ kumbhakaṃ vāpi bhajeccandrapathi sthite

puṣṭimṛtyujayādyarthaṃ svātmano 'nyasya recakam 
anagnijvalane vṛkṣaśoṣaṇe bījanāśane

stobhonmādaviṣoddīptipramukheṣu tu recakam 
dhyānārcanajapādyeṣu dehatyāge ca śāṅkare

kumbhako recakaśceṣṭo dīkṣāsaṃsthāpaneṣu ca 
yavīyān madhyamo jyeṣṭhaḥ sa tālairdvādaśādibhiḥ

tālo dvādaśabhirjānupariṇāhaparibhramaiḥ 
so 'pi dhyānajapopetaḥ sagarbho 'nyastadujjhitaḥ

yathā sagarbhaḥ sthairyāya manaso na tathetaraḥ 
prātarniśākṛtaṃ pāpaṃ dinānte ca divākṛtam

hantyagarbho 'pi devānāṃ pracalatvaṃ pradhāvatām 
snāto bhavati tīrtheṣu sarvakratuṣu dīkṣitaḥ

potaḥ pitṝṇāṃ yaḥ śaśvatsagarbhamimamācaret 
dhyāyedadhvāntagaṃ devaṃ japettadvācakaṃ sadā

ayamarkaguṇaṃ kālaṃ kṛtacittavyavasthitiḥ

prāṇāyāmaṃ vināpyevaṃ vaśyātmā cetasi sthitaḥ

mahāsurā devavarāś ca rājan ||

śūlaiś ca śūlāni samāhatāni @

khaḍgāś ca khaḍgaiḥ samam āhatā nṛpa |

kuntaiś ca kuntā yudhi yodhavīrāḥ @

samāhatāḥ saṃprati rājamānāḥ ||

śarān samāhatya śarāḥ samāhatā @

bāṇān samāhatya tathaiva bāṇāḥ |

rathī rathaṃ yāti balān mahāraṇe @

gajī gajaṃ yāti sa kuntapāṇiḥ |

aśvī hayaṃ yāti suśīghrahastaḥ @

sādī padātiṃ sphuritādharoṣṭhaḥ ||

khaḍgī ca hatvā yudhi yuddhavīraṃ @

nanāda khaḍgena sa khaḍgapāṇiḥ |

pāśena badhvā samare tu pāśī @

pāśaṃ dadhānaṃ sphuritādharoṣṭhaḥ ||

gajena hatvā gajam eva kevalaṃ @

gajādhipe khaḍgavareṇa pātite |

ākṛṣya dantau ripum anyam āśu @

jaghāna tābhyāṃ vinanāda bhūyaḥ ||

kaścid dhayaṃ sādinam ugravīryo @

hatvā tadīyaiś ca kuṭhārakuntaiḥ |

anyaṃ samāhatya tadīyam āśu @

jagrāha śastraṃ samare mahābalaḥ ||

gajāś ca kecit patitā mahāraṇe @

hayāś ca saṃbhinnaśirodharāḥ kṣitau |

rathāś ca kecid grathitograśaktayaḥ @

bhaṭāś ca kecin mathitākṣakūbarāḥ ||

tataḥ sapāśāḥ pracaranti yuddhe @

piśācasaṃghā atha rākśasottamāḥ |

gṛdhrā balāḥ śyenakapotakāś ca @

vikṛṣya māṃsāni nadanti sarve ||

utkṛṣya cākṛṣya ca māṃsarāśiṃ @

pibanti kāmaṃ rudhiraṃ śavānāṃ |

saṃchidya māṃsāni bahūni saṃkhye @

sthānaṃ vibhajyātha piśācasaṃghāḥ ||

tataś ca tūryāṇi bahūni saṃkhye @

nādaṃ mahāntaṃ prasabhaṃ pracakruḥ |

śaṅkhāś ca bheryaḥ paṇavāḥ saḍiṇḍimāḥ @

cakruḥ praṇādaṃ bahuśaḥ samāhatāḥ ||

saṃkṣobham īyuḥ sahasā ca lokāḥ @

kṣubdhāś ca sarve jalarāśayaś ca |

samāgame daityamahāsurāṇāṃ @

divaukasāṃ yuddhasamutsukānām ||

tasmin sughore mahati pravṛtte @

raṇottame devamahāsurāṇām |

dvandvāni yuddhāni babhūvur atra @

devāsurāṇāṃ jayam icchatāṃ nṛpa ||

na hy antarikṣaṃ na diśo na bhūmir @

na bhāskaro 'dṛśyata reṇujālaiḥ |

vavuś ca vātās tumulāḥ sadhūmā @

diśaś ca sarvās timiropagūḍhāḥ ||

nānāmaṇistambhasahasracitram @

āruhya yānaṃ dadṛśe svayaṃbhūḥ |

subhāsvaraṃ bhūtasahasrayuktaṃ @

pradīpyamānaṃ vapuṣā pareṇa ||

sutaptajāmbūnadacārucitram @

ānandabherīśatasaṃpraṇādam |

nakṣatracandrāṃśubhir aṃśumantaṃ @

vaidūryasomārkavibhūṣitāṅgam ||

tam ātmajo vai pulahaḥ pulastyas @

tathā marīcir bhṛgur aṅgirāś ca |

ṛksāmabhiḥ samyag abhiṣṭuvantaḥ @

sevanti devaṃ varadaṃ vimāne ||

taṃ pāvakā lokaguruṃ svayaṃbhuvaṃ @

sāṅgāś ca vedā makhadevatāś ca |

sevanti devaṃ bhuvaneśvareśaṃ @

bhūtāni cānyāni mahānubhāvam ||

ete ca bhūyaś ca manuṣyasaṃghā @

vaikhānasāḥ pāvakayonayaś ca |

sarve yayur devapurohitāś ca @

yuddhotsukāḥ sarvasurāsurāṇām |

yogeśvarāḥ ṣaṭ ca divākarābhā @

vāgbhūṣaṇair bhūṣitasarvadehāḥ |

jagatpatir devapatir janārdanaḥ @

śikhī ca cakrī śaraśārṅgadhanvā |

antarhito mādhava eva sākṣād @

draṣṭā raṇaṃ yāti nagendram īśaḥ |

antarhitā vai dadṛśur nabhaḥsthā @

nārāyaṇaś caiva naraś ca devaḥ ||

vaktraiś caturvedadharaiś caturbhiḥ @

saṃpūrṇacandrapratimaiḥ sukāntaiḥ |

sarvā diśo nistimirāś cakāra @

navodito 'sau śaradīva candraḥ ||

bhidyatīva nabhasthalam |

daityānāṃ ca surāṇāṃ ca @

te tadā devadānavāḥ |

yuddhaṃ pracakrire ramyaṃ @

pūṣāpi daityaṃ navabhiḥ śarai

vivyādha hemāṅgadabhūṣitāṅgakaḥ |

nirbhidya dehaṃ ditijasya te śarā @

jagmuḥ pṛthivyāṃ rudhirāruṇānanāḥ ||

meruṃ sūrya ivāṃśubhiḥ |

hatair dānavamukhyais tu @

viṣṇor amitatejasaḥ |

nārāyaṇasya balavān @

sa vahniḥ śāmitas tadā |

līḍhā pītāsi ca prabho |

tvam ante prāṇinām atsi @

sarvāṃl lokāṃs trīn imān havyavāha @

prāpte kāle pacasi tvaṃ samiddhaḥ |

tvam evaikas tapase jātavedo @

nānyas taptā vidyate goṣu deva ||

vṛṣākapiḥ sindhupatis tvam agne @

mahāmakheṣv agraharas tvam eva |

viśvasya bhūmnas tvam asi prasūtis @

tvaṃ ca pratiṣṭhā bhagavan prajānām ||

sṛjasy apo raśmibhir jātavedas @

tathauṣadhīr oṣadhīnāṃ rasāṃś ca |

viśvaṃ tvam ādāya yugāntakāle @

sraṣṭā bhavasy ānala sargakāle ||

tataḥ prayāntaṃ tridaśendrasaṃnidhau @

mahāsurendraṃ balim uttamaśriyam |

tam añjasā cakrur abhipradakṣiṇaṃ @

dvijāś ca mukhyāḥ paśavaś ca maṅgalāḥ ||

mahājaṭābhāradharās tapasvinas @

tadā tam āhur vidhimantramaṅgalaiḥ |

abhiṣṭuvantaḥ kavayaḥ svalaṃkṛtaṃ @

baliṃ prayāntaṃ raṇamūrdhani sthitam ||

jayāya mantraṃ kavayas tv alaṃkṛtaṃ @

baliṃ saṃetyocur athāgrataḥ sthitam |

prataptajāmbūnadacitrabhūṣaṇair @

divyaiś ca ratnair vividhair alaṃkṛtaḥ |

virājamānaḥ parameṇa varcasā @

raṇe vibhāty agniśikheva dānavaḥ ||

sa vai tadā śatrubalārditaṃ balaṃ @

balir dadarśottamavīryavikramaḥ |

jalāgame śrīmad ivābhramaṇḍalaṃ @

vikīryamāṇaṃ nabhasīva vāyunā ||

tato dadarśātha balāni sarvato @

raṇe suguptāni hutāśanena vai |

samutthitāny uttamavīryavanti @

samudravegān iva parvasaṃdhiṣu ||

sa śūlaśaktyṛṣṭigadāsisāyakān @

kṣipan ripūṇāṃ samudagratejasāḥ |

nanāda siṃharṣabhamattanāgavaj @

jalāgame toyadavac ca vīryavān ||

divyāstradhūmaḥ subhujaughavāyur @

mahābalaḥ pauruṣavikramendhanaḥ |

prajā didhakṣann iva kālavahniḥ @

sughorarūpo vibabhau raṇe baliḥ ||

darśanaṃ vo bhaviṣyati |

kukṣiṃ praviṣṭe jagatāṃ nivāse @

surendramātuḥ suralokavandye |

kṣubdhāś ca daityāḥ sakalā mahābalāḥ @

kṣobhaṃ samāyānti jagadgurau harau ||

bharadvājaḥ kaśyapo gautamaś ca @

viśvāmitro jamadagnir vasiṣṭhaḥ |

yaś codito bhāskaro vipranaṣṭe @

so 'py atrātrir bhagavān ājagāma ||

suvarṇaśṛṅgo hi mahānubhāvo @

lokeśvaro vāyumayo mahātmā |

svargekṣaṇaḥ kāñcanagarbhagauraḥ @

sa viśvayoniḥ paramo hi medhyaḥ ||

āsthāya vai vājinam āśvamedhikam @

iṣṭvā narā duṣkṛtam uttaranti |

yam āhur vai vedavido dvijendrā @

vaiśvānaraṃ vājinam āśvamedhikam ||

yathāśramāṇāṃ pravaro gṛhāśramo @

yathā nṛṇāṃ pravarā brāhmaṇāś ca |

yathāsurāṇāṃ pravaro bhavān iha @

tathā kratūnāṃ pravaro 'śvamedhaḥ ||

natāḥ sma bhūtādhipam ādidevaṃ @

jagajjanitraṃ sakalasya jantoḥ |

parāvareśaṃ paramaṃ vareṇyaṃ @

namo namaḥ satyajagatpate te ||

viśvasya dhātāram aṇīyasām aṇuṃ @

vedāntavedyaṃ param āmananti |

tam ādidevaṃ varadaṃ vareṇyaṃ @

natāḥ sma taṃ jagatāṃ patim ādibhūtam ||

natāḥ sma he bhūpatim ādidevam |

jaya bhāvana vāmana |

samakṣaṃ sarvabhūtānāṃ @

pātāle vasatā prabho |

śāsanaṃ tava viśveśa @

aśrotriyaṃ śrāddam adhītam avratam @

adakṣiṇaṃ yajñam anṛtvijā hutam |

aśraddhayā dattam asaṃskṛtaṃ havir @

ete pradattās tava daitya bhāgāḥ ||

baliḥ praviṣṭo 'tha rasātalaṃ mahad @

daityā gatāḥ sāgaram eva śeṣāḥ |

svasthaṃ jagat sarvam idaṃ munīśvarāḥ @

kriyāḥ kriyatāṃ mahatīs tapodhanāḥ ||

śrutvā tu bherīravam ādimūrter @

ājñākṛtaṃ sāgaraghoṣakalpam |

lokāś ca sarve sacarācarāś ca @

svasthānam āyānti bhayaṃ vimucya ||

śatāśvamedhasya yad atra puṇyaṃ @

catuḥsahasraṃ ca śatakratoś ca |

bhaved anantaṃ harivaṃśadānāt @

prakīrtitaṃ vyāsamaharṣiṇā ca ||

yad vājapeyena tu rājasūyād @

dṛṣṭaṃ phalaṃ hastirathena cānyat |

tal labhyate vyāsavacaḥ pramāṇaṃ @

gītaṃ ca vālmīkimaharṣiṇā ca ||

pārāśaryavacaḥ sarojam amalaṃ gītārthagandhotkaṭaṃ @

nānākhyānakakesaraṃ harikathāsaṃbodhanābodhitam |

loke sajjanaṣaṭpadair ahar ahaḥ pepīyamānaṃ mudā @

bhūyād bhāratapaṅkajaṃ kalimalapradhvaṃsi naḥ śreyase ||

vaśīṃ vibhūśitakarān navanīradābhāt @

pītāmbarād aruṇabimbaphalādharoṣṭhāt |

pūrṇendusundaramukhād aravindanetrāt @

kṛṣṇāt paraṃ kim api tattvam ahaṃ na jāne ||

idaṃ mayā te parikīrtitaṃ mahac @

chrīkṛṣṇamāhātmyam apāram adbhutam |

śṛṇvan paṭhann āśu samāpnuyāt phalaṃ @

yac cāpi lokeṣu sudurlabhaṃ mahat ||
